Running into a problem? This page walks through the most common issues Suno users encounter and how to resolve them. If none of these solutions work, contact the support team at [support@suno.com](mailto:support@suno.com).

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="My song doesn't sound like what I described">
    Vague prompts often produce unpredictable results. Here are the most effective ways to get the output you're imagining:

    - **Be more specific.** Instead of "a sad song," try "a melancholic indie folk ballad with fingerpicked acoustic guitar and male vocals, about missing home." Add details about instrumentation, tempo, mood, and vocal style.
    - **Use Custom Mode.** Custom Mode lets you write your own lyrics and specify a more detailed musical style, giving you much greater control over the output.
    - **Try a different model version.** If you're on Pro or Premier, make sure you're using v5.5, which follows prompts most accurately.
    - **Adjust Creative Sliders.** Use the creative controls to dial in intensity, structure, and variation to better match your intent.
    - **Generate multiple times.** Each generation is different — running the same prompt again often produces a noticeably different result.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Generation is taking too long or appears stuck">
    Long generation times usually come down to queue load:

    - **Free plan uses a shared queue.** During peak hours, Free plan users may wait longer as the queue fills up. This is expected behavior.
    - **Check your connection.** A dropped connection can cause the generation status to stall in your browser. Refresh the page — your song may have already finished generating and will appear in your Library.
    - **Try again later.** If the queue is congested, waiting a few minutes and trying again typically resolves it.
    - **Upgrade for priority access.** Pro and Premier subscribers use a **priority queue**, which significantly reduces wait times during busy periods.

    If a generation appears permanently stuck (more than 5–10 minutes with no update), refresh the page and check your Library. If the song isn't there, try generating again.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I ran out of credits">
    Here's what to do depending on your plan:

    - **Free plan:** Your 50 credits refresh automatically every day. Wait for the daily reset, shown in your account.
    - **Pro or Premier:** Your monthly credits refresh on your billing date. Check your account page to see your renewal date.
    - **Need credits sooner?** Purchase **add-on credit packs** from your account page on any paid plan. Add-on credits don't expire and carry over each month, so they're a good option for heavy usage periods.

    <Note>
      Add-on credits require an active Pro or Premier subscription to use. If your subscription lapses, add-on credits are held but inaccessible until you resubscribe.
    </Note>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I can't download my song">
    If the download option isn't working or you don't see WAV as an option:

    - **WAV download requires Pro or Premier.** Free plan users can only download in MP3. If you need lossless audio, upgrade your plan.
    - **Check your plan status.** If your subscription recently expired, WAV downloads will be unavailable for new songs. Songs created during an active subscription can still be downloaded in the format available at the time.
    - **Try a different browser.** Browser extensions (particularly ad blockers) can sometimes interfere with downloads. Try downloading in an incognito window or a different browser.
    - **Re-open the More Actions menu.** If the download fails silently, close the menu and try again.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Stems aren't separating correctly">
    Stem separation is a technically complex process and results can vary:

    - **Use Advanced Split for better results.** The Advanced Split option applies more detailed processing and typically produces cleaner separation than the standard split.
    - **Try Regenerate.** If the stems sound off, regenerating the original song with a slightly adjusted prompt and then splitting again can help.
    - **Some audio characteristics are harder to separate.** Songs with heavily layered production, reverb-drenched vocals, or instruments that overlap in frequency are more difficult to isolate cleanly. This is a known limitation of stem separation technology generally.
    - **Check your plan.** Pro includes 2 stem types; Premier includes 3 stem types. Free plan users do not have access to stem separation.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="My audio upload isn't working">
    If you're having trouble uploading an audio file for features like Cover or audio-referenced generation:

    - **Check the file format.** Suno supports common audio formats. Ensure your file is MP3, WAV, or another supported format — FLAC or unusual formats may not be accepted.
    - **Check the file size and length.** Files that are too large or too long may fail to upload. Free plan audio uploads are limited to **8 minutes**. Trim or compress your file if needed.
    - **Try a fresh upload.** Cancel the upload, wait a moment, and try again. Occasional upload failures are often resolved by simply retrying.
    - **Check your internet connection.** A slow or interrupted connection can cause uploads to time out.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I can't access Suno Studio">
    Suno Studio is a **Premier-only** feature. If you're on Free or Pro, you won't see Studio in your workspace.

    To access Studio, upgrade your plan to **Premier** from the account or billing page. Premier also includes additional features like MIDI export and 3-type stem separation.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Song Editor features are grayed out">
    Several advanced editing features in the Song Editor require a paid subscription:

    - **Replace section, Add section, and other structural edits** require **Pro or Premier**.
    - **Free plan** users have access to basic playback and generation but not advanced editing tools.

    If you're on Pro or Premier and still seeing grayed-out features, try refreshing your browser or signing out and back in to re-sync your account status.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="My lyrics aren't being followed correctly">
    If Suno is ignoring or heavily modifying the lyrics you provided:

    - **Use Custom Mode.** Simple prompt-based generation doesn't guarantee specific lyrics. Custom Mode is designed for exact lyric input — paste your lyrics there for the most faithful output.
    - **Keep structural tags.** Use tags like `[Verse]`, `[Chorus]`, `[Bridge]`, and `[Outro]` to structure your lyrics. Without these, Suno may interpret the layout differently.
    - **Keep sections concise.** Very long verses or dense lyric sections may get truncated or restructured. Break long sections into smaller chunks with clear tags.
    - **Avoid special characters.** Unusual punctuation or formatting can confuse the lyric parser. Stick to plain text with standard punctuation.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I need help or can't find an answer">
    If your issue isn't covered here, here's how to get help:

    - **Help Center:** Visit [help.suno.com](https://help.suno.com) for detailed guides, tutorials, and an expanded knowledge base.
    - **Email support:** Contact the Suno support team directly at [support@suno.com](mailto:support@suno.com). Include your username, a description of the issue, and any relevant song IDs or screenshots to help the team investigate quickly.
    - **Community:** The [Suno Discord](https://discord.gg/suno) is an active community where other users and Suno staff can often help with questions, tips, and workarounds.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
