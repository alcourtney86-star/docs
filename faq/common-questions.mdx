New to Suno or just looking for a quick answer? This page covers the questions we hear most often. If you don't find what you need here, visit [help.suno.com](https://help.suno.com) or reach out to [support@suno.com](mailto:support@suno.com).

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is Suno, and who is it for?">
    Suno is an AI music generation platform that lets anyone create original, full-length songs from a text description. You describe the music you want — the genre, mood, topic, or style — and Suno generates a complete song with vocals, instruments, and production in seconds.

    Suno is for everyone: hobbyists who want to make music for fun, content creators who need background tracks, musicians looking for inspiration, and professionals who want to produce and release commercial music. No musical training or technical background is required.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I need music experience to use Suno?">
    Not at all. You only need to describe what you want in plain language — for example, "an upbeat indie pop song about summer road trips" — and Suno handles the rest. If you do have music knowledge, Custom Mode lets you specify song structure, enter your own lyrics, and fine-tune the output. But you can make great music on Suno without knowing a single music theory concept.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do credits work?">
    Credits are the currency for generating songs on Suno. Every generation costs **5 credits** and produces **2 song variations** for you to choose from.

    - **Free plan:** 50 credits per day (renews daily), no rollover.
    - **Pro plan:** 2,500 credits per month (renews monthly), no rollover for included credits.
    - **Premier plan:** 10,000 credits per month (renews monthly), no rollover for included credits.

    You can also purchase **add-on credit packs** on any paid plan. Add-on credits do not expire and carry over month to month. Add-on credits require an active paid subscription to use.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What's the difference between model versions (v4, v4.5, v5, v5.5)?">
    Suno releases updated AI models over time, with each version improving audio quality, instruction-following, vocal realism, and creative range.

    - **v4 / v4.5** — earlier generation models, available on the Free plan (v4.5-all). Good for exploration and casual use.
    - **v5 / v5.5** — the latest and most capable models, available on Pro and Premier. They produce noticeably higher quality audio, more accurate adherence to prompts, and more natural-sounding vocals.

    If audio quality and prompt accuracy are important to you, upgrade to Pro or Premier for access to v5.5.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use Suno on mobile?">
    Yes. Suno has a dedicated mobile app available for both iOS and Android. The app supports song generation, Library management, playback, and direct sharing to social media platforms like Instagram and TikTok. Download it from the App Store or Google Play.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How long does generation take?">
    Most songs generate in under a minute. Generation time can vary based on server load and your plan. Pro and Premier subscribers use a **priority queue**, which means faster generation times during peak hours. Free plan users share a queue with other free users and may experience longer waits when demand is high.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What genres does Suno support?">
    Suno supports an extremely wide range of genres — from pop, rock, hip-hop, jazz, classical, and electronic to more niche styles like lo-fi, hyperpop, bluegrass, bossa nova, K-pop, reggaeton, and many more. You can also blend genres and describe subgenres or specific sonic references in your prompt. If you can describe it, Suno can attempt it.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use my own lyrics?">
    Yes. Switch to **Custom Mode** when creating a song and paste or type your lyrics directly into the lyrics field. You can also use structural tags (like `[Verse]`, `[Chorus]`, `[Bridge]`) to tell Suno how to arrange the song. Custom Mode gives you the most control over both the lyrics and the musical direction.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What's the difference between Free, Pro, and Premier?">
    Here's a quick overview:

    | Feature | Free | Pro | Premier |
    |---|---|---|---|
    | Credits | 50/day | 2,500/mo | 10,000/mo |
    | Model | v4.5-all | v5.5 | v5.5 |
    | Commercial rights | ❌ | ✅ | ✅ |
    | Priority queue | ❌ | ✅ | ✅ |
    | WAV download | ❌ | ✅ | ✅ |
    | Stems | ❌ | 2 types | 3 types |
    | Suno Studio | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ |
    | MIDI export | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ |
    | Price | Free | $8–10/mo | $24–30/mo |

    Pro costs $8/month (billed annually) or $10/month (billed monthly). Premier costs $24/month (billed annually) or $30/month (billed monthly).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I get more credits?">
    - **Free plan:** Your 50 daily credits refresh automatically every day.
    - **Paid plans:** Your monthly credits refresh on your billing date.
    - **Add-on credits:** On Pro or Premier, purchase additional credit packs from your account page. Add-on credits don't expire and roll over month to month, but they require an active paid subscription to use.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use Suno offline?">
    No. Suno's AI generation requires an active internet connection and runs on Suno's servers. You cannot generate songs offline. However, once you've downloaded a song (MP3 or WAV) to your device, you can listen to the file offline like any other audio file.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What languages does Suno support for lyrics?">
    Suno can generate lyrics and vocals in many languages beyond English, including Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, Japanese, Korean, Chinese, and others. Specify the language in your prompt (e.g., "a French chanson about Paris" or "a Spanish pop song") and Suno will attempt to generate lyrics in that language. Results may vary by language, with English generally producing the most consistent output.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I delete my account?">
    To delete your Suno account, go to your **Account Settings** page. Look for the account deletion option in the settings menu. Be aware that deleting your account is permanent — all your songs, playlists, and credits will be removed. Download any songs you want to keep before initiating deletion. If you need assistance, contact [support@suno.com](mailto:support@suno.com).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I contact support?">
    You can reach the Suno support team at [support@suno.com](mailto:support@suno.com). You can also browse the help center at [help.suno.com](https://help.suno.com) for guides, tutorials, and answers to common questions. For community discussion and tips, join the [Suno Discord](https://discord.gg/suno).
  </Accordion>
