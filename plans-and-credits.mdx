Every song you generate in Suno draws from a pool of credits. This page explains how credits work, what each plan includes, and how to get the most from your subscription — whether you're on the free tier or ready to upgrade.

## How Credits Work

Credits are the currency of creation in Suno. Each time you click **Create**, Suno spends **5 credits** and returns **2 song variations** — two unique takes on your prompt for the price of one generation. This means:

- 5 credits → 1 generation → 2 songs to choose from
- 50 credits → 10 generations → 20 song variations

Credits are consumed at the moment of generation, regardless of whether you save or download the result. Editing an existing song (extending, replacing sections) also costs credits.

<Note>
  Monthly credits reset at the start of each billing cycle and **do not roll over**. If you haven't used all your credits by the end of the month, they expire. Add-on credits purchased separately are the exception — see [Add-On Credits](#add-on-credits) below.
</Note>

## Free Plan

The Free plan is the best way to try Suno and keep creating casually at no cost.

- **50 credits per day**, renewing automatically at midnight in your local timezone.
- Access to the **v4.5-all** model.
- Basic Song Editor functionality for simple edits and extensions.
- Songs generated on the Free plan **cannot be used commercially**. Suno retains certain rights to free-tier generations — check the [Terms of Service](https://suno.com/terms) for details.
- Generations enter the **shared queue**, which may be slower during peak hours.

## Pro Plan

Pro is built for creators who generate regularly and need commercial rights and higher-quality output.

- **2,500 credits per month** (roughly 500 generations or 1,000 song variations).
- Access to **v5.5**, Suno's latest and highest-quality model.
- **Commercial use rights** — you own the songs you create and can monetise them.
- **Priority queue** with up to 10 generations running simultaneously, so you're never waiting long.
- **Advanced Song Editor** including section-level editing, reordering, and replacement.
- **Persona voices** — create and reuse a consistent AI voice across songs.
- **Custom models** trained on your own audio.
- **Audio upload** up to 30 minutes for remixing and style transfer.
- **Stem extraction** — export 2 stem types (e.g. vocals, instrumental) as time-aligned WAV files.
- **Add-on credits** available for purchase when you need more in a given month.

**Annual billing: $8/mo** (billed as $96/year)
**Monthly billing: $10/mo**

## Premier Plan

Premier is for power users, professionals, and anyone who wants the full Suno experience — including the browser-based audio workstation.

Everything in Pro, plus:

- **10,000 credits per month** (roughly 2,000 generations or 4,000 song variations).
- **Suno Studio** — a browser-based generative audio workstation for detailed arrangement and layering, exclusive to Premier.
- **3 stem types** for stem extraction, plus full **MIDI export** for use in any DAW.
- **Early access** to new AI models and experimental features before they launch to other tiers.

**Annual billing: $24/mo** (billed as $288/year)
**Monthly billing: $30/mo**

## Annual vs. Monthly Billing

Paying annually saves you **20%** compared to month-to-month pricing. There's no difference in features between annual and monthly billing — you get the same credits, models, and capabilities either way.

| | Pro Monthly | Pro Annual | Premier Monthly | Premier Annual |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Monthly cost** | $10 | $8 | $30 | $24 |
| **Annual total** | $120 | $96 | $360 | $288 |
| **Savings** | — | $24/year | — | $72/year |

You can switch between monthly and annual billing from **Settings → Subscription** at any time. If you switch to annual mid-cycle, the change takes effect at your next renewal date.

## Add-On Credits

If you run out of credits before the end of your billing cycle, you can purchase add-on credit packs directly from **Settings → Credits**.

- Add-on credits are available on **Pro and Premier** plans only.
- Purchased credits **do not expire** — they carry over month to month indefinitely.
- Add-on credits require an **active paid subscription**. If your subscription lapses, purchased credits are paused until you reactivate.
- Add-on credits are spent only after your monthly plan credits have been exhausted.

<Tip>
  If you find yourself regularly buying add-on credits, it's worth comparing the cost to upgrading your plan. Premier's 10,000 monthly credits often work out cheaper per generation than topping up a Pro plan.
</Tip>

## How to Upgrade or Downgrade

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Click your profile avatar in the bottom-left corner of the Suno interface, then select **Settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Subscription">
    Select the **Subscription** tab to see your current plan, credit balance, and renewal date.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a new plan">
    Click **Upgrade** to move to Pro or Premier, or **Change Plan** to switch between tiers. Select annual or monthly billing and confirm your payment details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the change">
    Upgrades take effect immediately — your credits and features update right away. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period, so you keep your current plan's benefits until it expires.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  When you downgrade, your monthly credits adjust to the new plan's allowance at the next renewal. Any unused credits from the higher-tier plan expire at the end of the billing cycle. Purchased add-on credits are retained.
</Note>

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Do unused credits roll over?**
No. Monthly plan credits reset at each billing cycle and do not carry over. Only purchased add-on credits persist across months.

**Can I use free-tier songs in a YouTube video?**
Not for monetised content. Commercial use rights require a paid plan. See the [Terms of Service](https://suno.com/terms) for the full licensing details.

**What happens to my songs if I cancel?**
Your Library remains accessible after cancellation, but you lose access to paid features and your credit allowance drops to the Free tier's 50 credits per day. You keep any songs already generated.

**Can I share a plan with someone else?**
Plans are tied to a single account and are not shareable.
