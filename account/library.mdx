Your Suno Library is your personal catalog — every song you generate and every track you like from the Explore feed lives here. It's the central hub for organizing, downloading, and managing your music.

## Accessing Your Library

Click the **Library** icon in the left-hand panel to open your Library at any time. You'll see all your generated songs as well as songs you've liked from other creators.

## Song States in Your Library

Your Library contains songs in three distinct states:

- **Generated** — songs you've created yourself through any generation method.
- **Liked** — public songs by other creators that you've hearted from the Explore feed.
- **Playlist songs** — songs you've organized into a named playlist (generated or liked songs can both be added).

## The Song Page and More Actions Menu

Every song has its own dedicated page at a unique URL (e.g., `suno.com/song/[id]`). The song page displays the waveform, lyrics, and playback controls.

On any song — in your Library or in the Explore feed — open the **More Actions (...)** menu to access:

- **Download** — save the audio file to your device
- **Get Stems** — separate the song into individual tracks (Pro/Premier)
- **Remix** — remix the song as a new generation
- **Extend** — add more to the end of the song
- **Cover** — create a cover version
- **Remaster** — apply audio remastering
- **Share** — copy a link or share to social media
- **Delete** — permanently remove the song from your Library
- **Set Privacy** — toggle the song between Public and Private

## Downloading Songs

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the More Actions menu">
    Click the **...** menu on the song in your Library or on the song's dedicated page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Download">
    Choose **Download** from the menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your format">
    Select **MP3** or **WAV**. WAV (lossless) is available on **Pro and Premier** plans only.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Free plan users can download in MP3 format. Upgrade to Pro or Premier to unlock lossless WAV downloads.
</Note>

## Public vs. Private Songs

Control who can discover your music using the privacy setting on each song.

| Setting | Who Can See It |
|---|---|
| **Public** | Visible on the Explore feed; discoverable by all Suno users |
| **Private** | Only visible to you in your Library |

To change a song's privacy, open the **More Actions (...)** menu on the song and select **Set Privacy**.

<Tip>
  Private songs still have a unique shareable URL. If you copy and share that link, anyone with it can listen — the song just won't appear publicly on Explore.
</Tip>

## Liking Songs from Explore

When you browse the Explore feed and find a song you love, click the **heart icon** to like it. Liked songs are saved directly to your Library so you can find them again any time, separate from the songs you've generated.

To unlike a song and remove it from your Library, click the heart icon again on the song.

## Organizing with Playlists

Playlists let you group songs into named collections — useful for organizing by mood, project, genre, or anything else you need.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a playlist">
    In your Library, select **New Playlist** and give it a name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add songs to a playlist">
    Open the **More Actions (...)** menu on any song and choose **Add to Playlist**, then select the playlist.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access your playlists">
    Find all your playlists listed in the Library panel. Click any playlist to view its songs.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Searching and Filtering

Use the search bar at the top of your Library to find songs by **title**. You can also filter your song list by **date** to quickly surface recent or older creations.

## Deleting Songs

To permanently delete a song from your Library:

1. Open the **More Actions (...)** menu on the song.
2. Select **Delete**.
3. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

<Warning>
  Deleting a song is permanent and cannot be undone. Download the audio file before deleting if you want to keep a local copy.
</Warning>
