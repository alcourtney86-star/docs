Suno is an AI-powered music creation platform that transforms a plain-text description into a complete, original song — vocals, lyrics, melody, and full production — in under a minute. Whether you're a lifelong music lover who has never touched an instrument or a professional producer looking for a new creative spark, Suno gives you a direct line from imagination to finished audio.

## Who Suno Is For

Suno is built for anyone who hears a song in their head and wants to get it out into the world. That includes:

- **Casual creators** who want personalised music for videos, podcasts, or just for fun.
- **Content creators and filmmakers** who need original, royalty-free tracks without licensing headaches.
- **Musicians and producers** who want to prototype ideas, explore new genres, or collaborate with AI as a creative partner.
- **Educators and developers** building music-related products or experiences.

No prior music knowledge, instrument skills, or audio software experience is required.

## The Core Creation Flow

Every Suno song starts with a prompt — a sentence or two describing what you want to hear. Suno's AI reads your description and generates two distinct song variations, each complete with:

- Original **lyrics** matched to your theme
- **Vocals** that fit the genre and mood
- Full **instrumentation and production**, from the opening bar to the final note

The whole process takes less than a minute. From there you can listen, save, share, or keep refining.

## Key Capabilities

### Text-to-Song Generation

Type a prompt, click **Create**, and receive two unique songs. Use Suno's **creative sliders** — weirdness, structure, and style intensity — to nudge the AI toward more experimental or more conventional results.

### Song Editor

The Song Editor lets you work section by section. Add a bridge, swap out a verse, reorder sections, crop the track, or replace a part entirely — all without starting over from scratch.

### Suno Studio

Available on the **Premier** plan, Suno Studio is a browser-based generative audio workstation. Arrange, layer, and shape your track with professional-grade controls while staying entirely within the Suno environment.

### Stem Extraction

Separate a finished song into individual stems — vocals, drums, bass, instruments — as time-aligned WAV files. **Pro** accounts get up to 2 stem types; **Premier** accounts unlock up to 3 stem types plus MIDI export for direct integration with your DAW.

### Custom Models and Persona Voices

Train a custom AI model on your own audio to capture a specific sonic signature, or create a **Persona** — a consistent AI voice you can use across multiple songs. Both features are available on **Pro** and **Premier** plans.

### Audio Upload and Remixing

Upload up to 30 minutes of your own audio on paid plans and use it as the foundation for a remix. Suno analyses the source material and generates a new song that reflects its style, feel, or instrumentation.

### Mobile Apps

The Suno app is available on **iOS** and **Android**, so you can create, listen, and edit wherever you are.

## AI Models

Suno's generation quality is tied to the model you use. Newer model versions produce higher-quality audio, more accurate prompt interpretation, and better vocal performance.

| Model | Availability |
|-------|-------------|
| v4 | All plans (legacy) |
| v4.5 | Free plan and above |
| v5 | Pro and Premier |
| v5.5 | Pro and Premier (latest) |

**Free** accounts use the **v4.5-all** model. **Pro** and **Premier** subscribers have access to **v5.5**, Suno's most capable model. Newer models are released regularly, and Premier subscribers get early access to experimental versions before they roll out broadly.

## Credits

Every song generation costs **5 credits** and yields **2 song variations**. Credits vary by plan:

- **Free:** 50 credits per day, renewing daily.
- **Pro:** 2,500 credits per month.
- **Premier:** 10,000 credits per month.

Monthly credits reset at the start of each billing cycle and do not roll over. If you need more, add-on credit packs are available on paid plans — purchased credits never expire as long as your subscription is active.

For a full breakdown of what's included at each tier, see [Plans & Credits](/plans-and-credits).
