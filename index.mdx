## Get Started in 4 Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a free account">
    Go to [suno.com](https://suno.com) and sign up — or download the Suno app on iOS or Android. Free accounts receive 50 credits every day, enough to generate 10 song variations with no credit card required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Describe the song you want">
    Navigate to **Create** and type a prompt. Be specific: include genre, mood, instruments, and a lyrical theme. For example, *"an upbeat indie rock song about a cross-country road trip, electric guitar, driving drums."*
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate and listen">
    Click **Create**. Suno produces two unique song variations — each with full vocals, lyrics, and production — in under a minute. Play both and pick your favourite.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit, extend, or share">
    Open the **Song Editor** to add new sections, swap out a verse, or crop the track to the perfect length. When you're happy, download the finished audio or share a link directly from your Library.
  </Step>
</Steps>
